Our Story

Bloxd io was born from a simple vision: to create the perfect fusion of Minecraft's creative freedom and the fast-paced excitement of .io games. Launched in February 2021 by developer Arthur, our game has grown from a small experimental project into one of the most popular browser-based gaming platforms.

What started as a passion project has evolved into a global community of millions of players who share our love for voxel-based creativity and competitive gameplay. We believe that gaming should be accessible to everyone, which is why Bloxd io runs directly in your web browser without any downloads or installations.
